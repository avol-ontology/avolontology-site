About AVOL

AVOL™ (Adaptive Value Ontology Layer) is a neutral, inspectable value representation layer that translates already-present, quantifiable, non-price components of an option into a standardized, monetary-equivalent form under a declared evaluation context. AVOL performs deterministic conversion and aggregation only: it does not rank options, recommend choices, optimize outcomes, infer preferences, or predict behavior. Its outputs are canonical value representations — complete as representations and comparable by construction at the moment of emission — intended for use as inputs to downstream systems, human evaluation, and automated agents. All interpretation, comparison, decision-making, and action occur outside the AVOL boundary and are not attributable to AVOL.
